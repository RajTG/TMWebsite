About

We are a bunch of students from NIT Trichy. We are passionate about Communication and leadership skills. We are constantly communicating with people around us - at home, for work, school/college, while commuting, etc. We all know the significance of clear communication, of effectively conveying our ideas to others. Many of us are leaders or aspiring to be one. Effective communication is a key trait of successful people. That is what members at Toastmasters NIT Trichy learn (and help others with), during club meetings.
